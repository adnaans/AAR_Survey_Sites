About Us

We do stuff to help people. We like people. That's why we help them. This non-profit is directed to helping people because it is our desire to provide them aid.

Our nonprofit aids people by raising money for people in need. This money is stored safely in a corporate account. We go to people in need and do background research to determine the essentials that they don't have, and use the money to purchase these essentials for them.
